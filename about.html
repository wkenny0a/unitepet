Our Story

Over two decades of manufacturing excellence, delivering premium pet accessories to the world.

Who We Are

A Leading Pet Products Manufacturer

Established in 2001, Unite Pet Manufacture Co., Ltd. has grown rapidly over the past two decades to become one of the leading pet products supply manufacturers in China.

As a dedicated manufacturer of pet accessories for overseas customers, we supply a wide range of products including collars, leashes, harnesses, car seat covers, clothing, bowls, beds, retractable leashes, pet toys, ID tags, and much more — well sold to customers in over 40 countries worldwide.

Our commitment to timely after-sales service has helped us build a strong reputation and lasting partnerships with customers around the globe.
